About BwindiTours

BwindiTours is a company located in Kisoro district in the south western region of Uganda. It carries out tours in the Bwindi Impenetrable national park region and the closing areas.
Our humble are given tour in the forest to tour the mountain gorillas, birds, waterfalls, the forest itself and all thetourism features in the area. Also tourism through the pygmies of Uganda and their culture, traditions and dances. Mountain climbing also is a service provided.

Tourism Attraction

Gorilla Tracking

This is the main attraction of Bwindi Impentrable National Park. The park consists of over 1000 gorillas. Gorillas of this park are shared among three countries that is Rwanda, DRC and Uganda but having Uganda with the most number of these indignous species of mamals.
With these beautiful creatures nearing exitinction, the wildlife of Uganda decided to give them a free world of their own to protect them better and look after them well.
BwidniTours as a tour company helps tourists to visit, study and understand them with the help of very well skilled personnels hired. Tours are carried out both in vehicles and on foot.
