BwindiTours

BwindiTours is a company located in Kisoro district in the south western region of Uganda. It carries out tours in the Bwindi Impenetrable national park region and the closing areas.
Our humble are given tour in the forest to tour the mountain gorillas, birds, waterfalls, the forest itself and all the tourism features in the area. Also tourism through the pygmies of Uganda and their culture, traditions and dances. Mountain climbing also is a service provided.
